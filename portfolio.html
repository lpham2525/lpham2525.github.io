Manhattan Project

A showcase which reveals the stunning beauty of Manhattan, both concrete and natural, both dynamic beings and inanimate objects. Also a call to action for Manhattan's residents and visitors to aid the city in reclaiming and retaining this beauty for generations to come.

Project ARTICHOKE

The go-to place for teaching children (and adults!) the nutritional importance of consuming vegetables as well as tips and tricks to make the whole idea easier to swallow (pun intended). Includes recipes and different methods of presentation to make veggies the star of mealtime (and even snacktime!).

The Human Genome Project

A collaborative project across multiple disciplines to map the entire human genome. Here exists an interactive model and educational platform to teach youth and adults about the fascinating and complex world of human genetics.

Project BLUEBIRD

Ever wondered what colors mean in the avian kingdom? From the magnificent arrays of a peacock's feathers to the exotic colors of the Australian parrot, here is the illustrated and interactive glossary of the most colorful birds on the planet.
