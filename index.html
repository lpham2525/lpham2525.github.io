Biography

Born in Viet Nam, my family and I immigrated to the United States when I was three. As a child, I had many dreams for what I wanted to be when I grew up, including children's book writer, zoologist, mental health counselor, and gift wrapper (all those silky ribbons and colorful, crinkly wrapping paper!). As an adult, I did not end up working in any of those professions. Instead, I worked as a social worker for five years until I quit to study coding. Equipped with coding skills, I hope to become a junior developer and web designer who can create dynamic webpages that are as responsive and interactive as they are eye-popping and aesthetically bold. In my spare time, I love reading pop psychology books, baking square-shaped buttermilk biscuits, and drawing anime characters in extravagant costumes. I am also a fan of board games, foreign films, and Welsh corgis. Contact me if you'd like to know more!
