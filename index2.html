Biography

Born in Viet Nam, my family and I immigrated to the United States when I was three. As a child, I had a lot of dreams for what I wanted to be when I grew up, including children's book writer, zoologist, counselor, and working in the gift wrapping department of a store. In college, I studied psychology and social work. I worked as a medical social worker for the last five years, but recently quit to study coding. In my spare time, I love reading pop psychology books, baking square-shaped buttermilk biscuits, and drawing anime characters in fancy costumes. I am also a fan of board games, foreign films, and Welsh corgis. Contact me if you'd like to know more!
